Biography

In 2015, when no one thought about a team can overturn Naruto's empire, he with Vegeta and Gotenks appeared and had participated in one of the best matches in the AFC Manga League and take the king's crown in a very persuasive way. A year later, his team had created history not for only them but change the history of AFC Manga League, by proving they can beat the best team in that moment, Horans the Snitzels. Although had gone through ups and downs, he was considered one of the best players from the Asian League and till now, this fact doesn't change at all.

Like Naruto, he is an all-round player, but with his wonderful physicality, he had swept all the best defences and obstacles to achieve his dreams.
